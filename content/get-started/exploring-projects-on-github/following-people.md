When you follow people, you'll see their public activity in the activity view of your news feed. To follow someone, visit their profile page and click **Follow** under their profile image.

![Follow user button](/assets/images/help/profile/follow-user-button.png)

To stop following someone, visit their profile page and click **Unfollow** under their profile image.
